Hi, I am Ms. Brunella D'Anzi.
Welcome to my website!

I am a Ph.D. student in experimental particle physics at TIFR and a member of the CMS Collaboration at CERN. My research interests include measuring the properties b-physics and searching for beyond-the-standard-model particles in LHC data with machine learning algorithms. I am also involved in the R&D of new gaseous detectors to be used at future colliders.
